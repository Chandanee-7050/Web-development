Chocalate Chip Cookies

Prep Time: 15 minute

Cook Time: 10 minute

Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 350oF(180oC).
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add chocalate chips and stir well.
  4. Bake for 10 10 minute and until golden brown.
Ingrediants
Ingrediants Quantity
Flour 2 Cups
Sugar 1Cups
Chocalate Chip 1 Cup
